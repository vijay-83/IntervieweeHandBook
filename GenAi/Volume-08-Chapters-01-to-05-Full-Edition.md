## Chapter 3 – Tokens & Context Windows

### Token

Small unit of text processed by the model.

### Examples

```text
Hello World
```

May become multiple tokens.

### Context Window

Maximum tokens the model can process.

### Challenges

- Context limits
- Token cost
- Latency

### Interview Questions

1. What is a token?
2. Why is context window important?
